Dale Sawatzky

Singer-songwriter and FVMA Winner

Gallery Music Bio Home


Dale, a British Columbia native, is a singer-songwriter who writes and performs music in a variety of genres including blues, jazz and country. In 2019, Dale won a Fraser Valley Music Award for his entry in the contest's Blues category. To date, he’s released three solo albums, the most recent being “Natural Workings”, a vocal jazz album. Dale performs often in the Fraser Valley area of BC, Canada. His albums can be found on most music-sharing platforms.
